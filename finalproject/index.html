About

Deet's Place is a coffee shop on Virginia Tech's campus located below D2 in Dietrick Dining Hall. We serve hot and cold speciality coffee drinks, 57 types of teas, pastries, paninies, and ice cream. Our highest selling items are the Caramel Macchiatos and blueberry muffins.
